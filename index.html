Tarot Reading

Tarot reading is one of the oldest and most popular divinatory arts. Tarot is not “telling your fortune.” Tarot is an amazing way to verify information, gives much needed insight into your inner self, and brings new awareness about life circumstances.

Tarot reading involves using 78 cards that have predetermined meanings; it is the reader’s connection with the cards and intuition that ensures the correct cards are pulled. The tarot can answer virtually any question one may have.

Pendulum Dowsing

Pendulum dowsing is one of the simplest divinatory arts. A pendulum is a weighted object on a string used to channel energy via electromagnetic impulses from the aether. Dowsing can be amazing way to verify information.

Pendulum dowsing works best with questions that can be answered yes, no, or undetermined. Be advised, the future is not set in stone, divination will give the most likely future outcome based on current circumstances. Timelines change frequently, all future readings should be taken as a possibility, not a guarantee.
